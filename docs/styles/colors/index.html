Colors

OpenBMC GUI color is comprised of different ranges of blue. Any color combination within the palette must comply with at least WCAG AA standard contrast raios. Status cololrs are used to specifically focus on the health and status of the system. Use of the status color should be consistent where green represents 'Good', yellow represents 'Warning', and red represents 'Danger'.
